About MedStore

MedStore is a simple online medicine store created for learning and practice purposes. This project helps in understanding basic concepts of Django such as models, views, templates, URL routing, and form handling.

Users can create an account, log in, and contact us through a basic message form. The goal of this mini project is to keep everything easy and clean, suitable for college-level projects.
